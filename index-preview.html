Our Story

A Bakery Born from Passion

Sonali Cake's is a proudly women-owned bakery nestled in the heart of Mahalunge, Pune. Founded with a simple dream — to bring joy to every celebration through the art of baking — we have grown into a beloved local institution known for our authentic flavors and stunning cake designs.

We believe that every cake is more than just a dessert — it's a memory in the making. From birthdays to weddings, anniversaries to baby showers, we craft each cake with premium ingredients, fresh daily, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our specialty lies in blending traditional Indian flavors — like Mango Rabdi and Shahi Rasmalai — with modern cake artistry, creating experiences that are truly one of a kind.
